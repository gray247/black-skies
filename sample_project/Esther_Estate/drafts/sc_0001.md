Mara braced her shoulder against the rusted hatch and forced it closed before the next surge crashed through. The cellar lights guttered, painting the breaker labels in a sickly pulse.

She thumbed the radio dial until a burst of static sharpened into a clipped voice. A coordinates string repeated twice before the signal dissolved, leaving the walls vibrating with phantom words.
